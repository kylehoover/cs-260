Series: Stormlight Archive #2

Words of Radiance
Brandon Sanderson
Notes

Considering how long the books in this series are, you would think there would be parts you would have to trudge through until the pace picked up again. And yet, that has been nothing like my experience with this book. Every single page seems to be crammed full of importance. I often feel that I'm missing little bits of information that Sanderson is laying out. The complexity of it all melts my mind a little, but also gets me really excited. I think I picked up on a reference to Nazh lurking around Bridge 4. It's subtle, but I don't think I'm off on this one. Can't wait to find out more about him (although knowing Sanderson, it could be a while).
