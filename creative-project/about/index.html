Welcome to Bookmark!
A place to keep track of all your books, and to get fun,
completely unnecessary statistics about them
Set yearly reading goals, keep track of your progress,
and view a history of everything you have read
FAQ*
*Most functionality is not yet implemented. Development is currently in the mockup stage.
What pages currently have content?
The Home page, the About page, clicking the Add Book button, and clicking on Words of Radiance on the Home page.
What information is the home page showing me?
The home page gives you a summary of the books you are currently reading, or have already read, for the current year. Click on one of the books to get more information about that book. The home page also gives you some quick information on how much progress you've made toward your reading goal for the year, as well as a few fun stats.
Where can I view all of my books?
You can find your books by clicking on the Books tab at the top of the page. There you will find all of the books you have read, are currently reading, or that you want to read. You can also filter what books you want displayed by year, reading status, genre, etc.
What do the different icons on the books mean?
bookmark Currently Reading check_circle Read add_circle Want to Read
How do I update the information for one of my books?
Click on the books image. This will take you to a page with information just about that book, as well as the ability to add/remove/change that information.
